I'm Henry Maina, a multi-disciplinary professional operating at the intersection of data science, technology, design, and strategic leadership. In a world of deep specialists, I work across disciplines with uncommon fluency — and that synthesis, not any single skill, is what defines my practice.

As CEO of HM Analytics Agency, I lead a team that converts organizational data into strategic advantage for clients across East Africa. As Co-Founder and Director of Elite Traders, I apply that same precision-led mindset to procurement and supply chain — proving the thinking translates beyond data into operations and trade.

My foundation is a Bachelor of Science in Statistics from Meru University of Science and Technology — rigorous quantitative training that grounds everything I do in empirical thinking and evidence-based decision-making. From that base, my practice has grown to span web development, graphic design, and market research, pursued not as side interests but as integrated professional tools.
